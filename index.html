About School

Christ Jyoti School is an english medium co-educational sr. sec. school. It was established in 1971. It is a christian minority educational institute under the jurisdiction of Satna discosen society, Satna(M.P.) The current principal of this school is Rev. Sr. Sajna and the current manager of our school is Rev. Father. Anil VJ. The school is very active in cultural and physical activities as well. It has won many inter-school competitions where the very well trained childrens of the school performed and won prizes, trophies and certificates for school.
